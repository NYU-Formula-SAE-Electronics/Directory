
# Learning KiCad

### Phil's Lab Tutorial Series
- Don't just watch the videos, do all the steps done in the video on your own machine in KiCad. It will take some time since you'll have to pause the video a lot.
- [Part 1 - Schematic](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=O-zNn5k5Bn4)
- [Part 2 - PCB](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=igQWdVGZGpI)
    - Do a different shape than what Phil does in the video so you'll have to think about the layout and routing for yourself instead of just fully copying his design
- Send the final board project to Veikko for review

### Helpful Tips and Practices for PCB Design Overall
- [10 Tips for PCB Design](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kkc8BxL5Eo0)
- [Aesthetic PCB Design Tips (In Altium, but applies to KiCad as well)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D2UaRPkRExw)
    - More important than you might think, no board will go on the car unless it also looks good.
